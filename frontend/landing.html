Our story

It's time for healthcare that cares.

People of color everywhere deserve better healthcare. People of color are being pushed out of a system that misdiagnoses, dismisses, and sidelines them. justcare fills this gap, by connecting patients to healthcare providers at proven hospitals. We keep hospitals accountable through our rating system that specifically targets obstacles people of color face in the healthcare process. In the future that justcare envisions, it doesn't exist. Search. Evaluate. Just care.

Our values

Cultural sensitivity

How empathetic is the healthcare professional? Did they seem to treat you differently because of your ethnicity? Did they make comments about your race?

Hospitality

Did the healthcare professional address you in a warm manner or abrasively? DId you feel welcomed? Did the healthcare professional smile or offer refreshments?

Quality of care

Were you satisfied with your care? Did you recieve your care quickly? Did you feel your care was efficient?
