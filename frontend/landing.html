What we value

Cultural sensitivity

How empathetic is the healthcare professional? Did they seem to treat you differently because of your ethnicity? Did they make comments about your race?

Hospitality

Did the healthcare professional address you in a warm manner or abrasively? DId you feel welcomed? Did the healthcare professional smile or offer refreshments?

Quality of care

Were you satisfied with your care? Did you recieve your care quickly? Did you feel your care was efficient?
